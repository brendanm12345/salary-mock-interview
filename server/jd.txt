About the team
NexaTech's mission is to build the next generation of intuitive and user-centric digital products. We believe in a digital world where products and services are accessible, usable, and delightful. Our team comprises passionate designers, researchers, and strategists working collaboratively to shape the future of digital interfaces.
The Design Team at NexaTech is at the heart of product innovation. Our focus is not just on how products look, but also how they function and feel. We understand the nuances of user behavior, and we craft experiences that resonate with our target audience.
About the role
This role is based in our Seattle HQ. We offer relocation assistance to new employees.
As a UX Designer, you'll play a pivotal role in crafting the user journey across our suite of products. You'll collaborate with product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to ensure our products are intuitive and provide real value to our users.
In this role, you'll:
Lead design brainstorming sessions and ideation workshops.
Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs.
Conduct user research and usability tests to gather feedback and iterate on designs.
Collaborate closely with the development team to ensure designs are implemented accurately.
Stay updated with the latest UX trends and tools.
You might thrive in this role if you have:
3+ years of experience as a UX designer.
A strong portfolio showcasing your best designs and the thought process behind them.
Experience with design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
Strong understanding of design principles and user-centered design methodology.
Excellent communication skills to present and defend design decisions.
Ability to handle feedback and iterate on designs.
A collaborative mindset and experience working in agile teams.
